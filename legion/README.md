# The American Legion

Data for American Legion posts and state departments across the United States.

| File | Records | Description |
|------|---------|-------------|
| `local-posts.csv` | 9,199 | Individual American Legion posts |
| `state-departments.csv` | 51 | American Legion state-level departments |

Founded in 1919 after World War I. The American Legion is the largest wartime veteran service organization in the United States.
