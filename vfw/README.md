# Veterans of Foreign Wars (VFW)

Data for VFW posts and state departments across the United States.

| File | Records | Description |
|------|---------|-------------|
| `local-posts.csv` | 1,384 | Individual VFW posts |
| `state-departments.csv` | 50 | VFW state-level departments |

Founded in 1899 after the Spanish-American War. The VFW is one of the largest veteran service organizations in the U.S., serving veterans of overseas conflicts.
